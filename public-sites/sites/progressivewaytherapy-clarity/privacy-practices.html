Here’s what confidentiality looks like in practice.

All sessions occur via HIPAA-compliant video; recordings are never made, and notes live in an encrypted portal.

Portal messaging is encrypted end-to-end. Email is only used for scheduling reminders or when you explicitly request it.

Only you and Blanca access your clinical record unless you sign a specific Release of Information form.

We’ll review limits of confidentiality—risk of harm, abuse reporting, or court orders—before your first session so there are no surprises.
