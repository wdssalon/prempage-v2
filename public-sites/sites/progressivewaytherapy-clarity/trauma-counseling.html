Where trauma counseling focuses

EMDR & memory reconsolidation

We prep intentionally, choose resourcing images that resonate, and run EMDR protocols that honor your capacity so integration feels grounding—not overwhelming.

Liberatory trauma processing

We name religious trauma, state violence, medical harm, and identity-based oppression directly while centering your agency and cultural context.

Body-based stabilization

Orienting, bilateral stimulation, sensory kits, and pacing rituals help your nervous system relearn what safety can feel like day to day.

Rebuilding daily safety rituals

Together we design morning, workday, and bed-time structures—as well as boundary scripts—that keep you resourced between sessions.
