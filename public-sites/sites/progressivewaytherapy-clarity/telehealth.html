TELEHEALTH EXPERIENCE

What virtual sessions feel like from the first click to the closing check-in.

Before your first appointment you'll receive a secure link, guidance for creating a private space, and ideas for sensory tools to keep nearby. Join from your couch, car, or office—as long as you're in Texas and grounded somewhere that feels safe.

We kick off each meeting by checking in on your nervous system and making sure the tech is cooperating. From there we move into EMDR resourcing, ketamine-integration prep, or weekly processing work with plenty of breaks, screen adjustments, and shared visuals to keep fatigue low.

If Wi-Fi drops or audio glitches, there's always a plan B—phone call, secure chat, or a rescheduled slot—so telehealth never feels fragile. The technology is part of the container, not something you have to troubleshoot alone.

Between sessions you can message through the client portal, revisit shared notes, and download resources. Accountability, humor, and gentle challenge stay intact even when we're working from different cities.
