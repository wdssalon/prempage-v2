HOW IT WORKS

Frequently asked questions

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the consult form?

Within one business day. You’ll receive an encrypted message with available consult slots, a short list of clarifying questions, and tech tips so we can hit the ground running.

Do you offer in-person sessions?

Not at this time. Progressive Way Therapy is 100% telehealth for adults physically located in Texas. We’ll help you set up a private virtual space and troubleshoot tech so sessions feel grounded.

Can partners or supporters join?

Yes, with notice. Let Blanca know who will attend and we’ll handle intake paperwork plus relational goals ahead of time so everyone feels prepped.

What should I expect during EMDR or ketamine integration sessions?

We start with resourcing, nervous-system grounding, and goal setting. EMDR reprocessing or ketamine integration only happens after we build safety and establish the target memories or insights together.

Do you accept insurance?

Progressive Way Therapy is out-of-network. You pay at the time of service and can submit the superbill we provide to your insurer or HSA/FSA for reimbursement.

What if I need to cancel?

Please give 48-hours notice so we can offer the slot to someone else. Late cancellations or no-shows are billed at the full session rate.

Can we message between sessions?

Yes. Portal messaging is included in your session fee for quick check-ins, homework questions, or resource requests. For crisis support, we’ll supply emergency resources since Blanca is not an on-call provider.
