Your migration story holds resilience, grief, and hope.

Whether you arrived by choice, necessity, or still live between countries, you deserve therapy that respects your culture, language, and community ties. Sessions can happen in English or bilingual check-ins that honor your primary language. We weave in cultural rituals, faith or non-faith practices, and family roles so care fits your life. If you're supporting undocumented loved ones or carrying intergenerational trauma, we move at a pace that preserves safety while building coping tools.

  • Space to process fear around raids, policy changes, and public rhetoric without retraumatizing yourself.
  • Support navigating cultural code-switching, racism, xenophobia, and belonging in progressive yet still harmful spaces.

  • Coordination with trusted legal, medical, and community resources upon request.
