ANXIETY COUNSELING

Name the pressure, quiet your nervous system, and rebuild daily rhythms that actually fit.

Anxiety isn't just racing thoughts—it's the hypervigilance, doomscrolling, activist fatigue, and people-pleasing that keep you bracing for the next hit. You might be holding the weight of multiple identities, a demanding job, caregiving, and the reality of living in Texas when your values and safety aren't always honored.

During sessions we zoom out and name the systems at play while zooming in on what your body is doing in the moment. We co-create grounding rituals, pacing tools, and liberatory narratives that make room for rest, joy, and your preferred ways of coping. Humor, clear language, and steady consent checks stay baked in so therapy feels human, not clinical.

Everything happens online so you can stay in your safest space. Expect direct conversations, affirming reflection, and action steps that respect your boundaries, culture, and energy level.

Where anxiety therapy focuses

Panic and freeze cycles

Practice nervous system resets, slower breathing patterns, and body-based check-ins that help you stay present when adrenaline spikes or everything goes blank.

Work and activist burnout

Untangle perfectionism, boundary leaks, and urgency culture so you can set humane expectations with coworkers, organizers, and yourself.

Identity-based vigilance

Name the racism, queerphobia, misogyny, or immigration stress that keeps your shoulders up by your ears—and build responses that protect you without burning you out.

Sleep and decision fatigue

Create closing rituals, somatic cues, and decision-making frameworks that calm spirals at night and make daytime choices feel less punishing.

Hi, I'm Blanca. We tackle anxiety with honesty, compassion, and strategy.

Anxiety shows up differently for every client. Some folks are buzzing with intrusive thoughts; others feel completely numb. I pay attention to the intersections—race, gender, sexuality, immigration, reproductive decisions—because those realities shape how anxiety lands.

You'll get a therapist who names oppressive systems out loud, offers plain language, and doesn't shy away from direct feedback. We use humor, grounding, and psychoeducation to steady you while we figure out what relief looks like in your real life.

Whether we're building scripts for tough conversations, practicing somatic tools, or unpacking trauma responses, I'm right there with you. Our work is collaborative, affirming, and paced to match your nervous system.
