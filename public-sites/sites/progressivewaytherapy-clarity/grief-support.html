GRIEF SUPPORT

Grief isn't linear. Together we figure out how to live with what changed.

Loss hits in waves. Some days you're functional; other days getting dressed feels impossible. Maybe you lost a person, a pregnancy, a community, a version of yourself, or a future you counted on. Maybe the loss is ongoing—like a parent with dementia, a partner navigating addiction, or watching rights stripped away while you try to stay hopeful.

You don't need to package your grief neatly for me. We sit with the sorrow, the anger, the emptiness, and the relief that sometimes shows up, too. I center inclusive, progressive grief care—naming systemic harm, colonial legacies, and religious trauma that can complicate mourning.

We build rituals that make sense for your culture and identities, resource your body so you can sleep again, and create containers where you can memorialize and move forward without betraying what you lost.

We make space for every version of your grief.

You can show up numb, sobbing, or joking your way through the session. We follow what you need in the moment, not a script someone handed you. You get language to explain your grief to people who don't understand, and boundaries when their advice hurts more than it helps.

  • Check-ins designed to keep you within your window of tolerance while still feeling.
  • Somatic grounding and breathwork for the nights when grief lives in your chest.
  • Ritual planning, memorial ideas, and creative practices to honor what matters.

We dismantle shame around how long grief “should” take and focus on integrating loss into a life you can keep living.

How we support your grieving process

Death & bereavement

Hold funerals, anniversaries, and frustrating updates with compassion. We build rituals, communication plans, and coping tools for gatherings when family dynamics are complex.

Ambiguous & collective grief

Name the ache of estranged relationships, climate anxiety, political violence, or rights being stripped away—and channel that pain into sustainable action and care.

Pregnancy & reproductive loss

Process miscarriage, abortion, fertility changes, or choosing to remain child-free with someone who will not judge your story or pressure your timeline.

Identity & life transition grief

Grieve versions of yourself that no longer fit, careers that ended, or communities you left. We honor the change while supporting the future you're creating.

I'm Blanca, and I sit in the grief with you.

Grief deserves witnesses who won't rush you. I bring a decade of trauma-informed care, EMDR, and radical affirmation to support clients mourning loved ones, pregnancies, communities, and the sense of safety they once had. We don't try to “fix” grief—we befriend it, learn from it, and reduce the panic it brings to your nervous system.

I offer plain language, humor when you need levity, and spaciousness when silence feels necessary. Expect collaborative rituals, body-based grounding, and gentle accountability to keep you tethered to care.
