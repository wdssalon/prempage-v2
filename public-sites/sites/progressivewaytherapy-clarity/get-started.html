ONBOARDING OVERVIEW

We co-create a plan that respects your bandwidth, identity, and goals from day one.

Your first touchpoint is a 15-minute consult where we confirm telehealth logistics, answer urgent questions, and align on the work ahead. From there we pace intake paperwork, trauma questionnaires, and tech checks so you never feel rushed. Sessions blend grounding, EMDR prep, and values mapping depending on what you need most.

Expect direct conversation, transparent scheduling, and plenty of space to name concerns before we put anything on the calendar. If you want to bring partners, supporters, or medical collaborators into the loop, we’ll note that up front and plan for releases accordingly.

Every step is documented inside the HIPAA-secure portal so you can revisit reminders, invoices, and resources whenever you need them.

Quick reference checklist before your first call

1.  Verify Texas location.

Telehealth licensure means you must be in Texas during every session. If you travel, let us know so we can pause or adjust.

2. Prep your tech.

Test headphones, camera, lighting, and Wi-Fi. Have a backup hotspot or phone handy in case of glitches.

3. Gather comfort items.

Bring water, fidgets, weighted blankets, or anything grounding so the virtual space feels supportive.
