WHY EMDR

Process trauma without reliving every detail on repeat.

Trauma does not have to keep hijacking your sleep, your startle response, or your relationships. EMDR creates enough distance from painful memories so your nervous system can process them in bite-sized pieces, even when describing them out loud feels impossible.

Working together online, we use bilateral stimulation tools that translate seamlessly to telehealth--taps, guided eye movements, and visual prompts you can control from your own space. Sessions move at your pace, with grounding built into every round so you stay present and resourced.

As a progressive, trauma-informed therapist, I name the systemic pressures that shaped the story and keep checking for consent along the way. EMDR with me is collaborative, affirming, and focused on helping you feel steadier right now, not someday in the distant future.

EMDR helps untangle the patterns that keep looping.

Flashbacks and triggers

Body based anxiety

Identity based harm
