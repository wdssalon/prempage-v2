INVESTMENT DETAILS

Therapy is an investment in capacity, not just crisis response.

Rates are set to reflect specialized trauma training, inclusive care practices, and the prep work that happens outside the hour we meet. Blanca keeps caseloads intentionally small, which means you get thorough follow-up, customized resources, and flexibility when life shifts. If you need a lighter cadence or a shorter session length, we’ll talk about it during your consult and adjust together.

Progressive Way Therapy is an out-of-network practice. Many clients still receive reimbursement using a superbill—we’ll walk you through the steps so insurance admin feels manageable instead of overwhelming.

Using out-of-network benefits doesn’t have to be intimidating.

Most clients receive partial reimbursement once they submit a superbill. We’ll help you gather the right codes, ask the right questions, and file cleanly so you’re not navigating the system alone.

  • Call the number on the back of your insurance card and ask about “out-of-network outpatient mental health benefits.”
  • Confirm deductible, coinsurance percentage, and whether telehealth sessions are covered.
  • After each session, download your superbill from the portal and submit it directly to your insurer or FSA/HSA platform.
  • Track reimbursement timelines—most carriers pay within 2-4 weeks. Let us know if you need documentation re-sent.

If insurance isn’t an option, we can discuss cadence adjustments, community fund referrals, or sliding scale openings as they become available.
