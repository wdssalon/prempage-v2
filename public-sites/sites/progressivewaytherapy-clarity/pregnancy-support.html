Every pregnancy journey is unique. Therapy supports yours without agenda.

You might be pregnant, postpartum, considering pregnancy, grieving a loss, or unsure what you want. Maybe you're navigating fertility treatments, surrogacy, adoption, abortion care, or parenting after trauma. Whatever brought you here, you deserve a therapist who trusts your body, your choices, and your family structure.

We process medical trauma, reproductive coercion, and systemic barriers while building coping tools for physical and emotional shifts. Sessions weave in body-based grounding, values mapping, and partner or support-person involvement if you want it.

  • Normalize hormonal changes, mood swings, and identity shifts without blaming you.
  • Plan conversations with providers, loved ones, and workplaces when boundaries feel hard.
  • Design safety plans for birthing, abortion access, or postpartum support that reflect your reality.
  • Integrate rituals, cultural practices, or chosen family support that keep you grounded.

This is your space to be honest about joy, fear, ambivalence, or relief—without judgment.
