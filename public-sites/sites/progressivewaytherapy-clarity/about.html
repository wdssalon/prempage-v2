Meet Blanca Kleinfall, M.Ed., LPC

I'm Blanca—therapist, educator, and unapologetic advocate for progressive mental health care. I founded Progressive Way Therapy so Texans who have been minimized, misgendered, or asked to mask their identities elsewhere could finally exhale. My style is direct, compassionate, and deeply curious about the intersections that shape your story.

Across 10+ years of trauma-focused work I've supported survivors, activists, caregivers, and high achievers who have carried far too much alone. I believe therapy should deliver both relief and skills, blending evidence-based modalities with radical affirmation so you can navigate relationships, boundaries, and nervous system shifts with steadier footing.

What working together looks like

1. Reach out and share what you need.

Submit the online form to request a free 15-minute consult. I'll reply within two business days with scheduling options and any clarifying questions.

2. We'll connect for a collaborative consult.

During our video call we'll talk through your story, name the systems impacting you, and decide together whether we're a good fit. You can ask about approach, logistics, and what support looks like.

3. Start therapy with clarity and a plan.

If we move forward, you'll receive onboarding paperwork and tech details for telehealth. Each session blends grounding, processing, and actionable practices so you feel momentum between meetings.
