    Hi! I'm Blanca Kleinfall, an openly progressive-minded therapist who believes in creating authentic spaces for healing. I offer a safe environment for LGBTQ+IA, minorities/BIPOC, women, and non-faith-based adults who need someone who truly gets it.

    With 10 years of experience, I believe in keeping it real with my clients. You just worry about bringing your authentic self to our sessions, and we'll work on the rest together. I specialize in evidence-based treatments including EMDR, trauma-informed care, and psychoeducation.

    Specialized Treatments

    Advanced therapeutic modalities for comprehensive healing

    EMDR Therapy

    Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing for trauma recovery. This evidence-based approach helps process difficult memories and reduce their emotional impact.

    Ketamine-Assisted Therapy

    Innovative treatment for treatment-resistant depression and trauma. Combining medical supervision with therapeutic support for breakthrough healing.

    Trauma-Informed Care

    Every session is grounded in understanding how trauma affects the mind and body. We prioritize safety, choice, and collaborative healing.

    Culturally Responsive Therapy

    Therapy that honors your cultural background, identity, and lived experience. Especially supportive for marginalized communities and immigrants.
