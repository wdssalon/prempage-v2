    Hi! I'm Blanca Kleinfall, an openly progressive-minded therapist who believes in creating authentic spaces for healing. I offer a safe environment for LGBTQ+IA, minorities/BIPOC, women, and non-faith-based adults who need someone who truly gets it.

    With 10 years of experience, I believe in keeping it real with my clients. You just worry about bringing your authentic self to our sessions, and we'll work on the rest together. I specialize in evidence-based treatments including EMDR, trauma-informed care, and psychoeducation.

    Working Together, Step by Step

    From the first consult to ongoing sessions, here's what you can expect when you choose Progressive Way Therapy.

    Stage One

    Connect & Clarify

    Start with a free 15-minute video consult. We explore what's bringing you in, any previous therapy experiences, and the kind of support that would feel most restorative now.

    • Liberatory intake questions that honor identity and context
    • Transparent conversation about rates, schedule, and expectations
    Stage Two

    Stabilize & Strategize

    Early sessions focus on building safety, gathering history, and identifying nervous system cues. Together we craft a plan that balances processing with skill-building.

    • Personalized resourcing toolkit for between-session support
    • Collaborative goals grounded in your capacity and values
    Stage Three

    Deep Work & Integration

    When you're ready, we lean into trauma processing, identity reclamation, or relational repair—all at your pace with plenty of grounding.

    • EMDR protocols, inner child work, and narrative reframing
    • Check-ins that prioritize consent and emotional safety
    Stage Four

    Sustain & Celebrate

    As healing takes root, we anchor in rituals, boundaries, and community care so you can keep thriving beyond therapy.

    • Graduated session cadence with optional booster check-ins
    • Referrals to affirming practitioners and community resources
