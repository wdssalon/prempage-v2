KETAMINE-ASSISTED THERAPY SUPPORT

Inclusive care that keeps your ketamine work meaningful.

You're investing in medically supervised ketamine because you want relief that talk therapy alone hasn't unlocked. But after the dosing session ends, you're left holding intense insights, big emotions, and sometimes a little wobble. My job is to make sure you never have to navigate that alone.

Instead of chasing quick fixes, we slow down and listen to what your nervous system is telling you. Preparation sessions clarify your intentions, surface trauma triggers, and build grounding plans so the medicine meets you in a supported container.

During integration, we translate the imagery, sensations, and revelations into everyday practices. We check for systemic harm, inherited narratives, and intersectional stressors that could derail your progress—and we name them out loud.

You'll leave each telehealth session with practical actions, psychoeducation, and radical affirmation. No spiritual bypassing, no judgment—just structured care that respects your whole identity and collaborates with the medical team overseeing your dosing.

We turn psychedelic breakthroughs into sustainable nervous system shifts.

Ketamine can open doors quickly, but integration requires a therapist who can hold intensity, identity, and logistics at the same time. In our telehealth space, you stay in your own environment while we build safety cues, crisis plans, and pacing strategies that match the dosing protocol your clinic is using.

Here's what we keep front and center:

  • Active coordination with prescribing physicians, infusion clinics, or KAP facilitators so information flows both directions.
  • Somatic grounding, EMDR-informed resourcing, and practical rituals to anchor insights in your body.
  • Safety planning for difficult journeys, including consent-driven support people, aftercare contacts, and emergency protocols.
  • Liberatory, affirming language that validates queer, trans, BIPOC, immigrant, reproductive, and neurodivergent experiences.

I do not prescribe or administer ketamine; I provide the therapeutic container around it. Sessions are fully virtual for adults located anywhere in Texas, and we can loop in partners or trusted supports when you need additional accountability.

How ketamine support begins

1.  Book your consult.

Schedule a free 15-minute consult right here to share your medical team, dosing plan, and access needs. We'll confirm licensing fit and map out support frequency.

2. We'll co-design your care map.

Your first 60-minute prep session sets intentions, clarifies medications, and outlines grounding tools. We coordinate with your clinic so everyone works from the same plan.

3. Integrate and reassess.

Integration sessions happen within 24-72 hours of each treatment, then as needed. We adapt rituals, track outcomes, and decide when to taper or add maintenance care.
