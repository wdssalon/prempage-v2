I'm happy you're here.

Hello! I'm Sarah. My methodology extends past conventional counseling conversations. I guide clients to address the core of their struggles. I focus on supporting stressed executives and partners who are facing relationship difficulties, career demands, and insecurity to interrupt patterns of rumination and counterproductive behaviors, develop inner confidence, and build strong, satisfying relationships.

I enter each session with genuine interest and empathy. I'm dedicated to empowering my clients so that when they conclude therapy, they feel self-assured in their capacity to make aligned choices in their personal and relational worlds. I'm convinced that existence becomes more rewarding and meaningful when we have faith in ourselves and can cultivate self-kindness.
